Andrade PWP

Purpose

This project will be a professional portfolio with a focus on the study of humanities in Albuquerque, NM.

Audience

This site is designed to showcase a digital portfolio of studies to higher education administrators. Target audience includes graduate school administrators, professors, and other higher education professionals.

Goal

The primary goal of this site is showcase public digital history in the form of images and writing to prospective clients, professionals, and graduate school administrators. The secondary function is to create an easy networking tool for interested parties to connect to my professional and academic network.

Persona

Name: Dr. Cartwright ALot; PhD. Yale University

Title: Chair of History Department; Yale University

Interests: History, Art, Literature in print.

Profession: As the chair of the History Department Dr. ALot approves and structures curriculum for all undergraduate and graduate students at Yale University. Dr. Alot has been a tenured professor for over 25 years and has been the chair of the history department for 12 years.

Technology: Uses iPhone 15 Pro and Safari browser to access the world wide web. Dr. ALot uses a MacBook Pro. Using Microsoft Office suite along with the safari web browser as it syncs with the browser on his phone.

Attitudes and Behaviors: Dr. Alot has a full schedule; meeting with other administrators and other staff members. He also an open door policy that allows students to schedule or talk. He very strapped for time but is very involved at the selection of the graduate students he allows on staff. He goes through applications thoroughly and is personally involved in the process. He spends time reading application packets and likes to find student that will be diversity to the student population and staff.

Frustrations and Needs: Dr. Alot wants to be able to strengthen the University by providing a diverse study body of graduate students to not only learn but work with staff in a productive and fruitful manner.

Goals: Find great applicants that will bring a large body of knowledge to the History department. Accessing digital history easily showcasing not only clear and concise research methods; but also the ability to showcase work digitally through complete comprehension of front-end web development methods.

User Story: As a user I can figure out this students methods, and go through his research portfolio determining whether to accept him into a graduate program on my iPhone.
